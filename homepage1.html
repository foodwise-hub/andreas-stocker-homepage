Background

What I actually do

I'm an accreditation and quality professional working across the social and human services sector, with a practical specialty in COA (Council on Accreditation) standards review and the systems used to track and report on it — Salesforce Lightning for accreditation management, and Power BI reporting built on SharePoint data. Most of what I do comes down to one question: does this program actually do what it says it does, and can we prove it?
